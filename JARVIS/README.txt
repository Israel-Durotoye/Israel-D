Market Price Estimator App

The Market Price Estimator App, developed by JARVIS for the MATLAB Nigeria Coding Challenge 2024, provides valuable insights into agricultural market prices based on historical data. 

The app enables users to import datasets and analyze market trends, regional performance, and price forecasts for various commodities. With features that include visualizations of price trends, regional price analysis, and market forecasting, this tool is designed to help farmers and sellers make informed decisions to optimize profit and sustainability.
